Controls:

Use UP, DOWN, LEFT and RIGHT arrow key or W, A, S and D key to control block. X key will make block down to floor directly.
Or you can click on the game to control. Click on Top, Left, Right and Buttom parts will move block as well as press keyboard.

Every button has a shortcut key which is the underline character on it.
If you think the music is noisy, click "Mute" button.


One more thing:

Tetris5 has been played times.
And totally scored points.
Just enjoy it!

iOS users use two fingers to scroll down or up.
